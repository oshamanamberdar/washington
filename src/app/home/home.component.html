Washington Connection offers a fast and easy way for families and individuals to apply for a variety of services such as Food, Cash,
Child Care, Long-Term Care, and Medicare Savings Programs. Individuals that are age 65 or older, blind or disabled may also apply for
medical assistance. Click “See If I Qualify” to get started. Watch Video

Washington Healthplanfinder offers healthcare coverage for children, parents/caretakers with children, pregnant women, or adults age
18 to 64 years old. For information regarding available services or to apply for benefits, visit www.wahealthplanfinder.org.
